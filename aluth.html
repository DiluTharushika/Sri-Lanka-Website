About Sri Lanka

Sri lanka,historically known as Ceylon and officially the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, is an island country in south Asia.It lies in the Indian Ocean,southwest of the Bay of Bengal,separated from the Indian peninsula by the ulf of mannar and the palk strait.

Sri Lankan Foods

Sri Lanka is not just the land of pristine beaches and unorthodox tea, but also the land of palatable curries and rice. Sri Lankan cuisine is similar to South Indian food, yet it has its own distinctive taste and flavours that canâ€™t be ignored. The impact of years of colonization and strong influence from different countries can be seen in the blend of different dishes, curry concoctions, and unique types of breads: Theti Paan and Roast Paan served in Sri Lanka.

Traveling places

Sri Lanka, a gem in the Indian Ocean, offers tourists a plethora of enchanting travel destinations. Beaches offer sun-kissed shores and vibrant coral reefs for those seeking relaxation or water adventures. The lush tea plantations provide a refreshing escape with their cool climate and scenic landscapes. With its diverse offerings, from cultural wonders to natural beauty, Sri Lanka promises an unforgettable travel experience for every tourist.

Traditional Festivals

Sri Lanka's traditional festivals offer tourists a colorful and culturally rich experience.The Esala Perahera in Kandy, Sinhala and Tamil New Year, Vesak,Thai Pongal..etc.These festivals not only showcase Sri Lanka's deep-rooted traditions but also offer an immersive and festive way for tourists to connect with the country's cultural heritage.
